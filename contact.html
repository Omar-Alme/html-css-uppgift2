about-us

We started our business in 2000 and already then we knew that we wanted to position ourselves as a shop that offers the best coffee and gadgets at the best prices. In addition to this, we have also put a lot of focus on personal service. ALKMISTEN is a high-quality coffee shop in Gothenburg. Our passion is what made us start the store, and it is also what makes us constantly develop. Among other things, we have coffee beans from Varanini, which come from Milan in Italy. If you want to know more about us or get better coffee at work, the restaurant or cafes, you can check out the website, visit the store, email or call! We look forward to meeting you.
