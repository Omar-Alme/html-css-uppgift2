Our Story

Since opening five years ago, Alkemisten has not only influenced Gothenburg's coffee culture but also the entire country's. We serve traceable specialty coffee with passion, and together with our dedicated baristas, we've built a community of coffee lovers. From humble beginnings to winning awards at the Swedish Gastronomi Prize and Swedish Roasting Championship, our journey has been one of growth and excellence.
